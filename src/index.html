About The Company

Silver Electronics has been involved in to the manufacturing of wide range of consumer utility products since its inception in 1995. The products are highly acclaimed for their superior performance and premium quality. The product list comprises of Small LED Torch, Silver SDH LED Torch Flashlight, Silver Boss LED Torch Flashlight, Small Rechargeable Laser LED Torch etc. Our company is also providing a great variety of consumer durables which includes plastic appliances and other daily utility consumer durables. The products are well known for their superior quality and durability and has helped us to earn a long list of satisfied customers. The products offered are very essential in our daily activities and form an essential part of our regular routine. Whether it is office or house, one can easily find these high quality products quite helpful.

With the broad vision and exemplary professionalism of Mr. Mansukhbhai Patel,Mr. Mukeshbhai Patel and Mr. Manibhai Patel The organization is persistently touching new dimensions. The company is known for its genuineness, integrity and quality in every task performed from their end.
